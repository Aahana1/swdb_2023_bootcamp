Drifting gratings#

The drifting gratings stimulus consists of a sinusoidal grating that is presented on the monitor that moves orthogonal to the orientation of the grating, moving in one of 8 directions (called orientation) and at one of 5 temporal frequencies. The grating has a spatial frequency of 0.04 cycles per degree and a contrast of 80%.
